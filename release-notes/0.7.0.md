## GridSense 0.7.0

- Paste straight from Excel, Google Sheets, Numbers, or LibreOffice: cells containing newlines and quotes now paste correctly (spreadsheets wrap such cells in quotes — the grid unwraps them instead of shredding rows), and Windows/unicode row separators are handled. No importer needed.
- New ＋ header at the right edge of the grid: click, type a property name (with autocomplete over properties used in this folder and across your vault), Enter — the column is added to every note, undoable. Also available with autocomplete in the columns manager.
- Fixed: keyboard shortcuts (undo, fill down/right, find & replace) now work in pop-out windows.
